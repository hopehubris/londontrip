Key Tips

🎟Book in advance: Tower of London, Platform 9¾, Brit Movie Tours DW walking tour, and NHM timed entry. Some sell out completely.
⚠️Windsor closed Tues & Wed. Don't rearrange the day trip to a closed day.
🆓Free museums: British Museum, NHM, V&A, Science Museum, National Gallery, House of MinaLima, and National Museum Cardiff are all free.
🚇Oyster card — get one at the airport. Far cheaper than individual tickets on all Tube lines and buses.
🗺Cardiff prep: Download a Doctor Who filming locations map before the day trip — fan-made maps are free and excellent.
🏨Home Base: Travelodge London Covent Garden — 10 Drury Lane, WC2B 5RE. Walking distance to many central attractions.

Travel itinerary

(London, England — 13 Day Trip)

Hotel:Travelodge Covent Garden, 10 Drury Lane WC2B 5RE
Arrival:May 5, 2025
Travellers:Two — wife & daughter
Departure:May 18, 2025
Day 1
Monday, May 5 — Arrival Day
Morning:Flight arrives — transfer to Travelodge Covent Garden & check in
Afternoon:Pick up Oyster card · Rest & freshen up
Evening:Gentle walk along the Embankment or through Covent Garden · Dinner nearby
Day 2
Tuesday, May 6 — Museums & Harry Potter
Morning:British Museum — Rosetta Stone, Egyptian mummies, Assyrian reliefs (3–4 hrs, free)
Afternoon:House of MinaLima — free Harry Potter graphic design gallery in Soho
Evening:Dinner in Soho or Covent Garden
Day 3
Wednesday, May 7 — Castle & Diagon Alley
Morning:Tower of London — Crown Jewels, Beefeater guided tour, the ravens (book in advance)
Afternoon:Leadenhall Market — the Diagon Alley entrance from The Philosopher's Stone
Evening:Explore the City of London or riverside walk
Day 4
Thursday, May 8 — Doctor Who Day
Morning:Brit Movie Tours Doctor Who Walking Tour — central London filming locations (book ahead)
Afternoon:Forbidden Planet — 179 Shaftesbury Ave · massive Doctor Who section
Evening:The Who Shop — Upton Park · world-class DW museum & shop
Day 5
Friday, May 9 — South Kensington Museums
Morning:Natural History Museum — blue whale, dinosaur gallery (free — book timed entry)
Afternoon:Victoria & Albert Museum — art, design & culture (free)
Evening:Science Museum — flight hall & interactive exhibits, or dinner nearby
Day 6
Saturday, May 10 — Day Trip: Windsor Castle ⚠️ Closed Tues & Weds
Morning:Train from Waterloo or Paddington (~45 min) · Arrive Windsor
Afternoon:Windsor Castle — State Apartments, St George's Chapel, audio guide & grounds
Evening:Lunch in Windsor town · Return train to London
Day 7
Sunday, May 11 — Rest & Culture
Morning:Slow start · Leisurely breakfast near the hotel
Afternoon:National Gallery — Van Gogh, da Vinci, Rembrandt, Monet (free)
Evening:Southbank walk or explore Covent Garden
Day 8
Monday, May 12 — Day Trip: Cardiff (Doctor Who Country)
Morning:Train from Paddington to Cardiff (~2 hrs)
Afternoon:Roald Dahl Plass · Cardiff Castle · Walk Doctor Who filming locations
Evening:National Museum Cardiff (free) · Return train to London
Day 9
Tuesday, May 13 — Day Trip: Gloucester Cathedral
Morning:Train from London to Gloucester (~2 hrs)
Afternoon:Gloucester Cathedral — Hogwarts corridor cloisters from films 1 & 2 (free)
Evening:Optional: Cheltenham stroll · Return to London
Day 10
Wednesday, May 14 — Platform 9¾ & Museums
Morning:Platform 9¾ at King's Cross · house scarves & wands · Harry Potter Shop
Afternoon:Science Museum — if not visited on Day 5
Evening:Dinner in Bloomsbury
Day 11
Thursday, May 15 — Day Trip: Castle Day
Morning:Train to Leeds Castle (Kent, ~1 hr + shuttle) or Hever Castle (~1.5 hrs)
Afternoon:Full castle day — maze & falconry at Leeds, or Anne Boleyn's gardens at Hever
Evening:Return train to London · Rest
Day 12
Friday, May 16 — Flex Day
Morning:Optional: River boat to Greenwich — Royal Observatory & Prime Meridian
Afternoon:Notting Hill & Portobello Road Market — or revisit a favourite
Evening:Borough Market — London's finest food market, near London Bridge
Day 13
Saturday, May 17 — Final London Day
Morning:Last-minute sights or souvenir shopping near Covent Garden
Afternoon:Walk the Embankment — Westminster Bridge to Tower Bridge
Evening:Farewell dinner · Pack & prepare for departure
Day 14
Sunday, May 18 — Departure
Morning:Final breakfast · Check out of hotel
Afternoon:Elizabeth line to Heathrow · Check in for flight home
Evening:Depart London — safe travels! ✈️

# London Itinerary: May 5–19, 2026
**Home base:** Travelodge London Central Covent Garden, 10 Drury Lane, London WC2B 5RE.
## Production Notes
- This plan prioritizes geographic clustering over squeezing in everything at the earliest possible moment.
- Book timed-entry or tour slots first, then adjust the exact day if the operator schedule forces it.
- The schedule deliberately leaves May 18 as a make-up/recovery day. Do not give that day away too early.
- For live London transit, use Citymapper or TfL on the day; the route links below are planning links, not service guarantees.
- The Bridgerton walking tour is treated as a Bath day trip because the Brit Movie Tours listing found is Bath-based.

## Booking Priority
1. Sherlock Afternoon Tea.
2. Jack the Ripper Museum + 17:00 walking tour.
3. The Who Shop Museum, Thursday–Saturday only.
4. Doctor Who London Walk.
5. Bridgerton Bath walking tour, if you want to keep it.
6. Leeds Castle / Hever Castle tickets and rail logistics.
